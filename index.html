About Our School

The Knowledge Public School - Taha Campus

We are committed to providing quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares them for the challenges of the future. Our dedicated faculty and modern facilities create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

With our Roots and Wing campuses, we offer a comprehensive educational experience from Playgroup all the way to Matriculation. Our holistic approach focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and character in every student.

At The Knowledge Public School, we believe that every child has unique potential waiting to be unlocked. Our mission is to provide the guidance, resources, and environment needed for each student to soar to new heights.

Our Campus

ROOTS CAMPUS

Roots Campus

The Roots Campus is dedicated to our youngest learners from Playgroup to Grade 2. This campus features child-friendly facilities, safe play areas, and specially designed classrooms that stimulate early childhood development.

Our experienced early childhood educators create a nurturing environment where children build their first educational foundations through play-based and interactive learning.

WING CAMPUS

Wing Campus

The Wing Campus accommodates students from Grade 3 to Matric. This campus features science laboratories, computer labs, a library, sports facilities, and classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids.

Here, students engage in more specialized learning as they prepare for higher education and develop the skills needed for success in an increasingly complex world.
